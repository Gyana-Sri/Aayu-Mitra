##"AayuMitra"-Universal Health Assistant

Aayu Mitra is an intelligent voice-based healthcare assistant designed to provide quick and easy medical guidance to people of all ages. The application helps users identify common health symptoms, understand their possible severity, and receive immediate first-aid and home-care recommendations before consulting a healthcare professional.

The system supports multiple Indian languages, making healthcare information accessible to people from different regions and language backgrounds. Users can interact with the application using voice commands or by selecting symptoms manually.

Key Features

🔹 Voice-Based Interaction

Users can speak their symptoms directly to the application.
Supports multiple Indian languages including English, Hindi, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Tamil, Kannada, Gujarati, Punjabi, and Urdu.

🔹 Patient Profile Management

Records important details such as age, gender, weight, diabetes history, and available medical equipment.
Provides more personalized health recommendations based on user information.

🔹 Symptom Analysis

Allows users to report symptoms such as:
Headache
Fever
Chest Pain
Breathing Problems
Dizziness
High Blood Sugar
Bleeding Injuries

🔹 Risk Assessment

Evaluates symptoms and categorizes the condition into:
Low Risk
Medium Risk
High Risk
Helps users understand when immediate medical attention may be necessary.

🔹 Home Remedies and First Aid Guidance

Provides simple, easy-to-follow instructions for managing common health issues at home.
Offers practical advice while encouraging professional medical consultation when required.

🔹 Emergency Support

Includes an emergency assistance option for severe health conditions.
Helps users take immediate action during critical situations.

🔹 Visual Health Indicators

Uses a human body diagram to highlight affected body areas.
Makes symptom understanding easier for users.
Objective

The primary goal of Aayu Mitra is to bridge the gap between people and basic healthcare information by providing instant, multilingual, voice-enabled medical assistance. It aims to improve health awareness, guide users during minor health concerns, and encourage timely medical intervention during emergencies.

Disclaimer

⚠️ Aayu Mitra is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The recommendations provided are intended for informational purposes only. Users should consult qualified healthcare professionals for proper diagnosis and treatment of any medical condition.
